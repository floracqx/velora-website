Account

Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Velora account directly inside the app.

In-app steps

How to delete

  1. Open Velora.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

What happens next

Permanent removal

  • Your account access is removed.
  • Your saved birth profile and generated readings are deleted.
  • Your saved compatibility entries are deleted.
  • This action cannot be undone.

Subscriptions

Important note

Deleting your Velora account does not cancel an active App Store subscription. Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID account settings.

Need help?

Contact support

If you cannot access the app or need help with deletion, contact support@velorainsights.com.
