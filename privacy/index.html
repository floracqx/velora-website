Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Velora ("we," "our," or "us") is a self-awareness app that uses pattern-based analysis to provide personalized insights about your identity, timing, relationships, and personal growth. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password when you create an account.
  • Birth Details: Birth date, birth time (optional), and birth place (optional). These are used to generate your personalized chart and readings.
  • Partner Information: If you use the compatibility feature, you may provide a partner's name, birth date, birth time, and birth place.
  • Notification Preferences: If you enable reminders, we store your notification preference and the push token needed to deliver those reminders.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Which screens you visit, features you interact with, and general app usage patterns.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Crash Reports: If the app encounters an error, we collect diagnostic data to improve stability.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise GPS location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data.
  • We do not use facial recognition or biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Birth details Generate your personalized chart, archetype, phase, and insight readings
Account email Account management, password recovery, and essential service communications
Partner birth details Generate compatibility readings between you and another person
Notification preferences and push token Deliver daily insight reminders when you opt in
Usage data Improve the app experience and understand which features are most useful
Crash reports Fix bugs and improve app stability

We do not use your data to:

  • Sell to third parties
  • Display targeted advertising
  • Build profiles for external marketing

4. How We Store Your Data

  • Your data is stored on secure servers.
  • Birth details and chart data are associated with your account and encrypted in transit.
  • Passwords are stored using hashed and salted authentication credentials.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

5. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Geocoding providers accessed through Velora backend Location autocomplete for birth place entry Search query text (city/place name)
Apple App Store Subscription purchases and receipt verification Purchase transaction data
Firebase Analytics Product analytics and feature usage measurement Event data, device/app metadata
Expo Notifications / Apple Push Notification service Deliver opt-in reminder notifications Push token and notification delivery data
Sentry Error monitoring and crash reporting Device info, error details (no personal data)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your personal data to train third-party AI models.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View your birth details and chart information in the app's Profile section.
  • Correct: Update your birth details or display name at any time.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Export: Contact us at privacy@velorainsights.com to request a copy of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Velora is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Hashed and salted password storage
  • Token-based authentication with expiration

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of Velora after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@velorainsights.com
  • Support Email: support@velorainsights.com
  • In-App Support: Available in the app under Settings > Help & Support
